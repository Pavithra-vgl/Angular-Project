ABOUT US

Pet world is a retailer which sells animals and pet care resources to the public. A variety of animal supplies and pet accessories are also sold in our shop.
The products sold include: food, treats, toys, collars, leashes, cat litter, cages and aquariums.
Services we provide:

  • Hygienic care (such as pet cleaning) and aesthetic services (such as cat and dog grooming)
  • Tips on training and behaviour, as well as advice on pet nutrition.
  • Engraving services for pet tags, which have the owner's contact information in case the pet gets lost.
